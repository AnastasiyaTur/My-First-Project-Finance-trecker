is a program for housekeeping and family budget planning. It will help you control your household budget.

The main idea is the division of expenses (and income) into categories. This allows you to immediately see where and in what quantities the money is spent and plan the family budget more efficiently.

The system of categories is tree-like with unlimited nesting - each category can have subcategories, those, in turn, their own subcategories, and so on.

In the reports, you can visually see information about the movement of funds and analyze the overall picture of the household budget. In this way, all cash flows can be easily assessed.
